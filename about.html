About Gospelar

A modern Sunday School companion for the Gospel Faith Mission International — built so the lesson, the lyric, and the leaderboard are always in your pocket.

Our mission

To make Gospelar's Sunday School curriculum accessible to every member of every congregation, in the language they pray in, on the device they already carry — and to give Sunday School teachers the tools they need to lead their classes well, even where the network is patchy.

Our vision

A connected Gospelar — where a child in Ìbàdàn, a teen in Kano, a young adult in Lagos, and a mother in Abuja can open the same app and find this week's lesson, hear today's hymn, and see the same Word taught in their heart language.

Built for offline first

Many of our churches meet where the network is unreliable. Teachers can mark attendance and award marks without a connection — the app saves locally and syncs automatically the moment Wi-Fi or data is available again.

Four languages, one Word

Every screen, lesson title, and notification is translated into English, Yorùbá, Igbo, and Hausa. Switch instantly from Settings — your progress and subscription move with you.
