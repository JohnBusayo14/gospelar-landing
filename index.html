Inside the app

Everything you need for Sunday School

One app for students, teachers, and church administrators — designed for offline-first use anywhere in Nigeria.

📖

Weekly lessons

Every quarter's lessons across all four age groups — Children, Intermediate, Youth, Adult — translated into the language of your service.

🎵

Hymns offline

The full Gospel Hymn Book in your pocket. Search by number or first line, share with your class.

🎯

Quizzes & leaderboards

Reinforce each lesson with a short quiz. Friendly leaderboards keep teens and adults engaged.

👨‍🏫

Teacher dashboard

Mark attendance and award marks for memory verses and answered questions — works fully offline, syncs when you're back online.

⛪

Church admin

Pastors and superintendents see real-time engagement: who's attending, who's completing lessons, top learners by class.

🌍

Four languages

English, Yorùbá, Igbo and Hausa — every screen, lesson title and notification translated. Switch instantly from Settings.
